About Us

Technology has been at the forefront of human evolution since times immemorial and to enhance this ideology among students ... found the official tech club of BC roy Engineering College, Durgapur named iCoders. Our aim is to guide spradling students with lates technology and skills. Through our club students get access to multiple contest and also get a chance to boost up their knowledge with our weekly coding classes and frequent bootcamps. Today we have over 1000 students enrolled with us and have conducter over 40 events. This has been possible with the collaborative and inquisitive student members who are backbone for this growth. iCoders have a lot of plans and goals for coming year, here are some that we have lined up

Goals:

  • Strengthen the technical knowledge.

  • To help them perform in this competitive environment.

  • To improve the quality through spreading knowledge. By growing the competitive coding culture.

  • To train, educate, for forthcoming technological future.

  • To organize various coding competitions, workshops, and seminars to help students excel in coding.

  • The students would benefited from this club and will have fun while learning and doing projects.
